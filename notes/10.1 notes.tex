10.1 Right Triangle Trigonometry

1. The Six Functions
opp = opposite, adj = adjacent, hyp = hypotenuse
$$ \sin\theta=\frac{opp}{hyp} \quad \cos\theta=\frac{adj}{hyp} \quad \tan\theta=\frac{opp}{adj} $$
$$ \csc\theta=\frac{1}{\sin\theta} \quad \sec\theta=\frac{1}{\cos\theta} \quad \cot\theta=\frac{1}{\tan\theta} $$

2. Find All 6 Given a Triangle (Ex. 1)
legs 5, 12 → hyp = 13
$$ \sin=\frac{12}{13} \quad \cos=\frac{5}{13} \quad \tan=\frac{12}{5} $$
$$ \csc=\frac{13}{12} \quad \sec=\frac{13}{5} \quad \cot=\frac{5}{12} $$

3. Find All 6 Given One Ratio (Ex. 2)
$\sin\theta = \frac{4}{7}$: opp=4, hyp=7, adj=$\sqrt{33}$
$$ \cos=\frac{\sqrt{33}}{7} \quad \tan=\frac{4}{\sqrt{33}} \quad \csc=\frac{7}{4} $$
$$ \sec=\frac{7}{\sqrt{33}} \quad \cot=\frac{\sqrt{33}}{4} $$

4. Special Angle Values
$$ 30°: \sin=\tfrac{1}{2},\ \cos=\tfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\ \tan=\tfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} $$
$$ 45°: \sin=\tfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2},\ \cos=\tfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2},\ \tan=1 $$
$$ 60°: \sin=\tfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\ \cos=\tfrac{1}{2},\ \tan=\sqrt{3} $$

5. Finding a Side (Ex. 3)
Set up trig equation with known angle and side, solve for x.
$$ \cos 30°=\frac{x}{8} \Rightarrow x=8\cdot\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=4\sqrt{3} $$

6. Solving a Triangle (Ex. 4)
B = 90° - 28° = 62°, b = 15
$$ \tan 28°=\frac{a}{15} \Rightarrow a\approx 7.98 $$
$$ \sec 28°=\frac{c}{15} \Rightarrow c\approx 16.99 $$

7. Angle of Elevation/Depression
elevation = angle UP from horizontal
depression = angle DOWN from horizontal
$$ \sin 28°=\frac{h}{72} \Rightarrow h\approx 33.8 \text{ ft} $$
